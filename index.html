CRO: More success for your Shopify store

" Conversion Rate Optimization " basically includes all measures with the aim of " converting " as many visitors as possible into paying customers.

Conversion Rate Optimization is a technique for increasing the percentage of your website traffic that makes a purchase, also known as a conversion.

Kevin Donnelly, Marketing Manager @ Shopify

There are different approaches to CRO. The basic principle, however, is always to understand psychological mechanisms in the purchase decision and to tailor the customer experience optimally to the target group (s) and the product or products.

Can the success of our measures be measured?

Yes, the impact of our CRO measures can be measured. With statements like " At Store XY we doubled the conversion rate from 1.4% to 2.8%!" we hold back on principle. Because: In practice, an extremely large number of factors influence the conversion rate and it is impossible to look at cause and effect in isolation.

Example: After the relaunch of one of our shops, the conversion rate doubled on mobile devices. It would be easy to record a conversion increase of 100% here.

The increased marketing measures, a more targeted control of Google Ads, an expanded product portfolio and the general increase in motivation in the team have probably also played their part.

So it's impossible to tell whether our CRO measures boosted conversion by 100%, 80% or 30%.
